# Privacy Policy for Prism AI

**Last updated:** January 2025

## Overview

Prism AI is a privacy-focused Chrome extension that helps protect your sensitive information when using AI chat services. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**Prism AI collects NO personal data.**

- We do NOT collect any personal information
- We do NOT track your browsing activity
- We do NOT send any data to external servers
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
- We do NOT share any information with third parties

## How It Works

All processing happens **locally in your browser**:

1. The extension analyzes text you type in AI chat interfaces
2. Pattern matching runs entirely on your device
3. Detection results are stored only in your browser's local storage
4. No data ever leaves your computer

## Data Storage

The only data stored is:
- **Detection statistics** (count of items detected) - stored locally in Chrome's storage
- This data never leaves your browser and can be reset at any time

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Why We Need It |
|------------|----------------|
| `storage` | To save your detection statistics locally |
| `activeTab` | To analyze text in AI chat interfaces you visit |
| Host permissions for ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini | To inject the privacy protection UI on these sites |

## Third-Party Services

Prism AI does NOT integrate with any third-party services. We have:
- No analytics
- No crash reporting
- No external API calls
- No advertising

## Open Source

Prism AI is open source. You can audit our code at:
https://github.com/vishwastam/prism-ai

## Children's Privacy

Prism AI does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
